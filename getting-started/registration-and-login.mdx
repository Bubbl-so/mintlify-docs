## Registration

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web">
    Follow these quick steps to join the Bubbl community and start discovering exclusive events near you.

    1. **Visit the Explore Page**

       Go to [Bubbl](https://bubbl.so/explore)
    2. **Click “Log In”**

       You’ll find the Log In button at the top right of the screen.
    3. **Enter Your Phone Number**

       We’ll send a one-time verification code to your phone via SMS.

       If you don’t receive the code, you can also click “Send via WhatsApp” as an alternative.
    4. **Enter the Verification Code**

       Check your messages and enter the code to verify your identity.
    5. **Complete Your Profile**

       Fill out your first name, last name, and email address.

       You also have the option to upload a profile picture to personalize your account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="App">
    Follow these quick steps to join the Bubbl community and start discovering exclusive events near you.

    1. **Download the App**

       Get Bubbl on iOS from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bubbl-life-offline/id1635236135)
    2. **Sign In**

       Use your Phone Number or Apple ID to create your account.

       If you don’t receive your SMS code, tap “Try via WhatsApp” for a quick alternative.
    3. **Enable Permissions**

       Notifications: Stay up to date with invites, ticket drops, and event updates

       Contacts: See which friends are already on Bubbl and get smarter recommendations
    4. **Add Your Friends**

       Connect with friends to view their in-app activity and discover events they’re attending.
    5. **Follow Top Communities**

       Be the first to know when your favorite organizers post a new event.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
